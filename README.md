# TeachersValidate Payments Tracker 🍎

Welcome to the **TeachersValidate Payments Tracker**! 

If you are an educator participating in edtech focus groups and 1-on-1 feedback sessions, this tool is built exclusively for you. We know you are busy, so we designed this tracker to be as fast and simple as possible. No spreadsheets, no accounting jargon—just a quick way to log your hours and track your $50/hr earnings.

---

## 🚀 How to Get Started

### 1. Sign Up
Go to our live website: [payments-tracker-one.vercel.app](https://payments-tracker-one.vercel.app)
- Click **Sign Up**.
- Enter your email and a secure password.
- Make sure you select **"I am a Teacher"** before you click the sign-up button.

### 2. Log a Session (The "Quick Log")
Just finished a Zoom call? Awesome! Here is how to log it:
- Look for the **"Quick Log After Zoom"** box on your dashboard.
- Click the date and the exact time the call happened.
- Click **"Focus Group"** or **"1-on-1"**.
- Click the button for how long the session was (e.g., **1 hr**, **2 hrs**).
- Click **Log**! 

*That's it! The system automatically calculates your earnings at $50/hr and saves the session.*

### 3. Track Your Payments
When you log a new session, it will show up in your Session History as **Pending**. This acts like an open invoice letting the edtech companies (Vendors) know that you haven't been paid for your time yet.

- **Total Hours & Earned**: At the top of your screen, you can always see your total hours tracked, how much money is currently pending, and how much has been successfully paid out.
- **Getting Paid**: Once a vendor sends your payment, they will mark the session as Paid. Your dashboard will instantly update, turn green, and show you the exact time the payment was confirmed!
- **Self-Management**: If you already received the money and want to clear it from your pending list manually, you can simply click the yellow "Pending" button yourself to instantly switch it to "Paid".

---

## 🏢 For Vendors / EdTech Companies
If you are an EdTech company paying teachers for their time:
1. Sign up on the website and select **"I am a Vendor"**.
2. You will be taken to a specialized Vendor Dashboard.
3. You can see a global feed of all pending teacher sessions and your total outstanding liability.
4. After you process a payment to a teacher, simply click the "Pending" badge next to their session to mark it as Paid. The teacher will see this update instantly on their end.
